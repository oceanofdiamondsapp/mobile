Return Policy

General Return Policy

We care about your satisfaction and stand behind every item we sell. Since every order is different, a Specific Return Policy applies to every order. You will be notified of the Specific Return Policy before submitting the order, and if full refund applies. Restrictions apply. Please contact our Customer Service Department on 732-855-9353 for more information.

Special orders

SPECIAL ORDERS CANNOT BE RETURNED. A PIECE THAT INCLUDES SPECIFIC PERSONALIZATION INCLUDING INITIALS, ENGRAVING, NAMES, CHARACTERS OR OTHER EMBELLISHMENT THAT IS PERSONALIZED CAN NOT BE RETURNED. AN UNMARKED RING PLUS CENTER DIAMOND THAT WE MOUNT TOGETHER IS NOT CONSIDERED A SPECIAL ORDER.

Improperly Packaged Items

We understand rings may need to be resized, and that is why we include free resizing on every ring order. Please follow the return instructions above, and follow the instructions on your delivery slip.

Additional Terms & Conditions

Items sent to our office without the original order number, packages shipped beyond the 30-day return period, and packages that are improperly packaged or uninsured will be refused receipt.

Damaged and Used Items

Returned merchandise must be undamaged and in a new and unused condition. Items showing obvious signs of wear, or alteration by a jeweler other than Ocean of Diamonds, will not be accepted.

Loose Diamonds

Loose diamonds must be accompanied by the original laboratory grading report or a $150 charge will be assessed to replace the certificate.
